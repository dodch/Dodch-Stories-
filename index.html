About Dodch Stories

✨ Here, stories are not just written — they are lived. Every word is a mirror of what we can’t say out loud, symbols of who we are, and who we might become. ✨

🌌 Our Storytelling Philosophy

We believe that everyone carries something special inside — a thought, a feeling, an idea, a perspective that only they can express. Here, we invite writers to bring those hidden parts of themselves to life.

A story on our site should not just tell events. It should:

  • Reveal what we can’t say with normal words.
  • Carry deep meanings, symbols, and hidden layers for readers to uncover.
  • Reflect the truth of our lives — sometimes directly, sometimes through metaphor or fantasy.
  • Show us what it feels like to be human in a way that is unforgettable.

⸻

✨ What Makes a Story Right for Us

• Modern, relatable, unique — connected to today’s life yet timeless in meaning.

• Emotional impact — it should stay in the reader’s mind long after they finish.

• Symbols & hidden meanings — every story is more than what’s on the page.

• Personal truth — your unique perspective, your special thing, should shine through.

⸻

🚫 What We Avoid

• Empty writing without purpose or depth.

• Copy-paste tropes or soulless stories.

• Work that doesn’t reveal anything new about life, humanity, or ourselves.

⸻

🌍 Why We Exist

We are not here for ordinary stories.
We are here for the stories that transform us — the ones that make us stop, think, feel, and see the world differently.

Everyone has something unique to share.
This is the place to bring it to life.
